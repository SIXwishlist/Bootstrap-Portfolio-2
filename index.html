About Me



Kacie transforms schools by helping teachers integrate computer science across their content area. She is shaping our future by providing students with skills they need for jobs that don't even exist yet. The curriculum she supports surrounds game-based learning and programming. Students learn block-based coding in Scratch, Lego WeDo, Lego Minestorms, and MakeCode. Students build and create with Lego robotics and Minecraft Education Edition. She is always in awe of children's creativity!

Kacie is a mom, maker, and wannabe baker. She loves looking for inspiration. From that, she makes and shares in hopes of inspiring others.
